## Company: CloudScale Technologies

**Location:** Berlin, Germany (Hybrid - 2 days office)  
**Employment Type:** Full-time, Permanent  
**Salary Range:** €70,000 - €90,000 + Benefits  
**Posted:** January 15, 2025

---

## About Us

CloudScale Technologies is a rapidly growing B2B SaaS company helping enterprises manage their cloud infrastructure efficiently. Our platform serves 200+ enterprise customers across Europe, processing billions of API requests monthly. We're backed by leading VCs and are expanding our engineering team.

---

## The Role

We're looking for a **Backend Engineer** to join our Cloud Platform team. You'll be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining the core services that power our infrastructure management platform. This is an opportunity to work on challenging distributed systems problems at scale.

---

## What You'll Do

- Design and implement scalable backend services using Python and Go
- Build and maintain RESTful APIs and event-driven microservices
- Collaborate with frontend engineers to define API contracts
- Optimize database queries and implement caching strategies
- Contribute to architectural decisions and technical roadmap
- Participate in on-call rotation for production systems
- Mentor junior team members and conduct code reviews
- Write comprehensive tests and documentation

---

## What We're Looking For

**Required:**
- 4+ years of experience in backend development
- Strong proficiency in Python or Go (we use both)
- Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred)
- Familiarity with containerization (Docker) and orchestration (Kubernetes)
- Understanding of RESTful API design principles
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices
- Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills
- Fluent in English (German is a plus)

**Nice to Have:**
- Experience with message queues (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Familiarity with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform)
- Open-source contributions
- Experience with observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana)

---

## What We Offer

- Competitive salary (€70K-90K based on experience)
- Equity participation (stock options)
- Flexible hybrid work model (2 days office, 3 days remote)
- 30 days vacation + public holidays
- Learning budget (€2,000/year for courses, conferences)
- Home office equipment allowance
- Team events and annual company retreat
- Modern office in Berlin Mitte with free snacks and drinks

---

## Tech Stack

- **Languages:** Python (FastAPI), Go
- **Databases:** PostgreSQL, Redis, Elasticsearch
- **Infrastructure:** Kubernetes, Docker, AWS
- **Tools:** GitHub, Terraform, Datadog, PagerDuty

---

## How to Apply

Send your CV and a brief cover letter explaining why you're excited about this role to: jobs@cloudscale.example.com

We review applications on a rolling basis. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for:
1. Initial screening call (30 min)
2. Technical interview with coding exercise (90 min)
3. System design interview (60 min)
4. Team fit conversation (45 min)
